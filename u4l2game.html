🔮 Predict the Cycle
Use the clues to predict where the economy is headed. This checks whether you can apply the business cycle, not just label it.
Scenario 1 · Expansion Warning
GDP is rising, businesses are hiring, and consumer spending is strong. The economy is still growing, but growth is starting to slow. What phase usually comes next?
Scenario 2 · Downturn
GDP has fallen for two consecutive quarters. Businesses are cutting costs, and layoffs are rising. What is this officially called?
Scenario 3 · Bottom of the Cycle
The economy is still weak, but the crash has stopped getting worse. GDP is no longer falling as sharply. Which phase is this?
Scenario 4 · Early Warning Signs
Stock prices fall, building permits slow, and unemployment claims rise before GDP confirms a recession. What are these warning signs called?
